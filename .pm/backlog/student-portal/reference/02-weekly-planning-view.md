## Goals (this week)

*There's nothing here you can't do — the question is how fast you do it. Each goal has two timelines:*

- **Committed** = realistic. You should hit this 90–100% of the time.
- **Stretch** = aggressive. You should hit this 50–80% of the time. If you're hitting stretch every week, your stretch timelines aren't ambitious enough.
